What is Haven's Edge?

Haven’s Edge is a cluster of ARK Survival Ascended servers that mix the thrill of PvP with the security of the Sanctuary — a safe zone inspired by the Continental Hotel where tribes can rest, trade, and store resources without fear of raids.

Features

  • ⚔️ Balanced PvP with Safe Zones
  • 🦖 Custom Vault Rooms & Player Housing
  • 💥 Boosted XP, Harvest, and Taming Rates
  • 🌍 Cross-map Travel and Sanctuary Access
  • 🎯 Player-run economy and vault rental system

Rules

  • 🛑 No PvP or griefing in sanctuary zones
  • 🤝 Respect other players & their property
  • 🚫 No blocking caves, obelisks, or resource areas
  • ⚠️ No cheating or exploit abuse — zero tolerance

Sanctuary System

The Sanctuary is a protected environment located in each map’s hub. Every tribe may rent a secure vault space or personal room, protected by admin enforcement and peace protocols. Think of it as a “Continental Hotel” — neutral ground for all.
